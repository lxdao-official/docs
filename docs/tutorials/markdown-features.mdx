# How to subscribe LXDAO Public Calendar

This is a tutorial for how to subscribe LXDAO Public Calendar.

## LXDAO Public Calendar in UTC Timezone

We have a Public Calendar in UTC Timezone:

<iframe
  width="100%"
  height="900px"
  src="https://outlook.office365.com/calendar/published/f0585d963f094edd80b7658b37c1e900@lxdao.io/9fde5e7dc864430b82ef05268521ec612834428632829516792/calendar.html"
/>

But this isn't very convenient to use, we also provide a better way to subscribe the calendar to your own calendar (Google Calendar, Outlook, Apple Calendar, etc). It will convert the time to your local timezone and send you notification automatically.

## How to subscribe LXDAO Public Calendar

The ICS is:

```
https://outlook.office365.com/owa/calendar/f0585d963f094edd80b7658b37c1e900@lxdao.io/9fde5e7dc864430b82ef05268521ec612834428632829516792/calendar.ics
```

Please subscribe it to your own calendar.

### (Recommended) If you are using Apple Calendar

1. Open the Calendar app on your Mac

   <img src="/img/applesubscribe.png" style={{ maxWidth: 400 }} />

2. Paste the ICS URL into the "URL" field and update "Auto-refresh"
   <img src="/img/applesubscribe2.png" style={{ maxWidth: 400 }} />
3. After that, you will be about to see LXDAO Public Events in your Apple Calendar

## There might be events delay in Google Calendar and Outlook as they might refresh the calendar every 24 hours or more

### If you are using Google Calendar

1. Go to [Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/r)
2. Click the "+" icon next to the "Other calendars" section, and click "From URL"
   <img src="/img/googlesubscribe.png" style={{ maxWidth: 400 }} />
3. Paste the ICS URL above into the "URL" field, and click "Add calendar"
   <img src="/img/googlesubscribe2.png" style={{ maxWidth: 400 }} />
4. After that, you will be about to see LXDAO Public Events in your Google Calendar

### If you are using Outlook

1. Go to [Outlook add Calendar](https://outlook.office.com/calendar/addcalendar)
2. Go to "Subscribe from web" and paste the ICS URL into the "URL" field and import
   <img src="/img/outlooksubscribe.png" style={{ maxWidth: 400 }} />
3. After that, you will be about to see LXDAO Public Events in your Outlook Calendar
